Our Story

Kenosha Welcome Center has been serving our community since 2017, providing essential services to those in need.

Our Mission

To provide friendship, food assistance, meals, showers, and emergency clothing to economically disadvantaged individuals and families residing in Kenosha County, Wisconsin.

Our Vision

A Kenosha community where every individual has access to essential resources and support for a dignified life.

Our Values

Dignity, Compassion, Community, Service, and Inclusivity guide everything we do.

Our Journey

February 2017

Our Beginning

Started with just 4 volunteers and 6 guests, initially planned as a 6-week event.

May 2019

Food Pantry Launch

Expanded services to include a weekly food pantry, serving up to 55 families each week.

2020

Pandemic Response

Adapted services to meet increased community needs during COVID-19.

2024

Independent Non-Profit

Established as an independent 501(c)(3) organization to better serve our community.
