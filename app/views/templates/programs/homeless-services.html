Homeless Services

Free help and resources for anyone experiencing homelessness in Kenosha County.

Kenosha Welcome Center provides immediate help with basic needs while connecting you to housing and other important services.

Coordinated Entry Housing Services

Housing Assessment & Referrals

Through Coordinated Entry, we work to connect you with local housing providers and programs based on your specific situation and needs. Our staff will help guide you through the assessment process.

  • Comprehensive housing assessment
  • Referrals to local housing programs
  • Connection to support services

Available Resources

Survival Items

  • Sleeping bags
  • Emergency blankets
  • Tents and tarps (when available)
  • Personal hygiene kits
  • Plastic bags for weather protection
  • And more items available
Items given out first come, first served while supplies last

Cold Weather Items

  • Winter hats and gloves
  • Hand warmers
  • Thermal underwear
  • Insulated winter coats
  • Warm winter socks
  • Winter boots

3-Day Food Packs

Pack Contents

  • Easy-open canned foods
  • Ready-to-eat snacks
  • Bottled water
  • Eating utensils included

Ready to Eat

  • No cooking needed
  • No microwave needed
  • No refrigeration needed

Distribution

  • Available during open hours
  • One pack per person
  • Weekly distribution allowed
